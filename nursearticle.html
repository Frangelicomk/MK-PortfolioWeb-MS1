Why and how I became a Nurse

I have been a nurse for the past 9 years, the journey was difficult but enjoyable. I studied Nursing in Athens, Greece for 4 years. I have been working in various settings and countries just so to get more experience.

Here i will cover the following :

  • Why i studied Nursing in the first place
  • When and Where?
  • My experience through this journey
  • Random facts about Nursing
  • Skills gained through the years
  • Why employee me as a Nurse?

Why i studied Nursing in the first place

My mother was a Nurse and she was working with elderly, I used to go often after school I would spend time with the elderly doing activities. Science with all its forms always got my attention. After high school I got into University and started my journey.

When and Where?

I started February 2016 in Athens, Greece at ATEI of Athens Department of Nursing

My experience through this journey

I gained experience by working while studying and after graduation I got my first job as a Nurse in the Netherlands. I moved from Greece to the Netherlands and believe it or not I had to create my owm limited company so it could operate as an agency. Started with just myself and it remained like that unfortunately as sortly after I moved in England. I moved in England back in August 2015 where I worked as a support care worker with Elite Care Services. This gave me the help I needed to get to know the system and how nursing homes and Hospitals work.

Random facts about Nursing

  • -The 12th May marks International Nurse's Day and this year is the 200th Anniversary of the birth of Florence Nightingale.
  • -On averange Nurses are walking 4 to 5 miles per 12 hour shift and for the record I do walk 7-8miles and that is in the same building
  • -Being a nurse is very satisfying job but not always that rewarding at the end of the day specially when you have to deal with death
  • -Nurses can be Doctors
  • -Get ready for constant training every year

Skills gained through the years

Written and communication skills. And I could also walk miles without getting tired. I gained various of skills and knowledge by working and studying a lot.

Why employee me as a Nurse?

Registered Nurse with 6+ years of nursing experience, 3+ years of experience as a Deputy Manager. I managed to build a strong relationship with the families of our residents and to deliver high quality care. Outgoing and detail oriented, proficient at building and maintaining professional relationships. I do have good knowledge of Dementia and high level of Wound care management.
